Features

Who are we really — a team of developers who create applications.
Some of our features are;

Online Seat Booking

Passengers can book seats in advance, ensuring a comfortable and organized journey. This feature reduces the hassle of on-the-spot booking and provides real-time seat availability, making travel planning easier than ever.

QR Code Scanner

With our integrated QR code system, payments and ticket validation are swift and contactless. Passengers simply scan a QR code, enabling a smooth boarding process and minimizing delays. This adds an extra layer of convenience and security to the ticketing experience.

Live Location

Our live location feature allows passengers to track the exact position of their bus in real-time. This transparency in transit gives passengers more control over their time, helping them manage their schedules with accurate arrival and departure information.

IOT Device

Our IOT-Enabled solution users rassbarry pi to desply QR codes in Dynemically.

about us

At RootX, Our Smart Digital Mobility Experience project is designed to revolutionize the way people interact with public transportation. By integrating QR code payment systems, real-time updates, and seamless user experiences, our platform simplifies and modernizes bus travel. Passengers can now enjoy faster, more secure transactions and get up-to-date route information, all from the convenience of their devices. With a focus on efficiency, security, and user-friendliness, this project aims to make public transit more accessible and appealing for everyone. Whether for daily commuting or occasional travel, the Smart Digital Mobility Experience combines the latest in digital technology with practical, real-world needs, setting a new standard for transportation systems.
